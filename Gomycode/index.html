About resume

Currently, I am a member researcher of research in the Laboratories Geosciences and Natural Waters Treatment at the Center of Research and Water Technologies in Borj Cedria (Tunisia). During my research period (my master and my thesis), I have seven years in the field of scientific research at the Research and Technological Center of Waters in which I was a researcher member at the Laboratories Geosciences and Treatment of Natural Waters and participation in three national and international projects. I have three years of experience in higher education (teacher at the Faculty of Science of Gafsa, University of Tunis) and the Private Higher School of Engineers and Technological Studies of Tunis (Arabic University Private of Sciences) The main tools used for my research are basically classical (field and laboratory) and modern mapping (computer modeling (ArcGis, ArcView) and modeling software (MidlandValley move (2D and 3D), Tectonics, structural geology, the creation of Digital Elevation Models (3DEM), use of different geophysical methods (gravimetry, seismic (wells (oil andwater drilling), reflection and refraction) and electric), conversion of geographical andkilometric coordinates (Geocalcs), creation of a Geographic Information System (GIS) for adata base. In addition, my training of geological, structural geology, analysis of fault and fold, deformation mechanisms and time-dependent rheology changes in sedimentary rocks, fault mechanics and fault reactivation assessments, and modeling of geological processes during rock deformation. This research experience, beyond my disciplinary and academic anchoring, has allowed me to develop my editorial skills over the years by participating in the preparation of reports, publications, and research. Your internship will enable me to develop my skills, anchor my know-how and prepare myself for the functions of a researcher.
